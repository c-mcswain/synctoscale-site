Employee Offboarding Template

Standardize secure employee offboarding across HR, IT, and Security. Automatically revoke access, notify stakeholders, and maintain a complete audit trail — with Nova surfacing gaps or risk signals.

Overview

The Employee Offboarding template coordinates secure deprovisioning when an employee leaves the organization. Once a termination event is recorded, SyncToScale revokes system access, triggers operational tasks, and logs every action for audit visibility.

How it works

  1. Termination is recorded in the HRIS.
  2. SyncToScale detects the event and builds an offboarding profile.
  3. Nova validates timing, approvals, and required fields.
  4. Identity and application access is revoked.
  5. IT and Security tasks are created automatically.
  6. Stakeholders are notified and actions are logged.

Workflow steps

  1. Step 1 — Trigger: HRIS termination event

    Fires when an employee status changes to terminated or inactive.

  2. Step 2 — Nova validation

    Nova checks termination timing, manager assignment, and effective dates.

  3. Step 3 — Access revocation

    Identity, SSO, and application permissions are disabled.

  4. Step 4 — Task creation

    IT and Security tasks are created in ticketing systems.

  5. Step 5 — Notifications & audit logging

    All actions are logged and key teams are notified.

Nova insights for this template

  • Flags late or retroactive termination entries.
  • Identifies access gaps across identity providers.
  • Detects repeat delays by department or manager.
  • Supports audit readiness and compliance reviews.
