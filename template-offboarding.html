Workflow Template

Employee Offboarding

A calm, dependable offboarding workflow that helps teams wrap up employment cleanly — coordinating HR, IT, payroll, and internal tools without dropped steps or last-minute chaos.

HR Operations Intermediate Termination Trigger HRIS · Payroll · IT · Slack

Overview

Offboarding is one of the most sensitive operational moments inside an organization. This template ensures employee exits are handled consistently, securely, and with full visibility.

Once a termination is recorded, the workflow quietly coordinates actions across systems while maintaining an audit-friendly trail.

Primary Triggers

  • Employee termination recorded in the HRIS
  • Scheduled separation date reached
  • Termination details confirmed or updated

Key Workflow Actions

  • Deactivate system access and credentials
  • Notify IT and security teams automatically
  • Trigger final payroll processing
  • Reassign owned documents and responsibilities
  • Log completion for audit and compliance tracking

Common Integrations

  • HRIS platforms
  • Payroll systems
  • Identity and access management tools
  • Slack or email notifications

Operational Notes

This workflow reduces manual follow-ups while still allowing teams to review or pause individual steps when needed.
