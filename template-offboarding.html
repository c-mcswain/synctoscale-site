Overview

Offboarding is one of the most sensitive operational moments inside an organization. This workflow ensures employee exits are handled consistently, securely, and with full visibility across teams.

Once a termination is recorded, SyncToScale coordinates actions across systems while maintaining a clear, audit-ready trail.

Primary triggers

  • Employee termination recorded in the HRIS
  • Scheduled separation date reached
  • Termination details confirmed or updated

Key workflow actions

  • Deactivate system access and credentials
  • Notify IT and security teams automatically
  • Trigger final payroll processing
  • Reassign owned documents and responsibilities
  • Log completion for audit and compliance tracking

Common integrations

  • HRIS platforms
  • Payroll systems
  • Identity and access management tools
  • Slack or email notifications

Operational notes

This workflow reduces manual follow-ups while still allowing teams to review, pause, or override individual steps when needed.

Workflow summary

  • Trigger: Employee termination
  • Owner: HR ¬∑ IT ¬∑ Payroll
  • Runtime: Scheduled / Event-based
  • Output: Access removal + audit log
