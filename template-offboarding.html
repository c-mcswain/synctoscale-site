Employee Offboarding Automation

A reliable offboarding workflow that quietly coordinates HR, IT, payroll, and internal tools to ensure clean exits, secure access removal, and accurate final pay — without last-minute scrambling.

HR Operations Intermediate Termination Event HRIS · Payroll · IT · Slack

Overview

Offboarding is one of the most sensitive operational workflows inside any organization. This template ensures exits are handled consistently, securely, and without relying on memory or manual checklists.

Once a termination is recorded, the workflow coordinates actions across people systems and internal tooling while maintaining a clear audit trail.

Primary Triggers

  • Employee termination recorded in the HRIS
  • Scheduled separation date reached
  • Termination details confirmed or updated

Key Workflow Actions

  • Deactivate system access and credentials
  • Notify IT and security teams automatically
  • Trigger final payroll processing
  • Reassign owned documents and responsibilities
  • Log offboarding completion for audit visibility

Common Integrations

  • HRIS platforms for employee status changes
  • Payroll systems for final compensation
  • Identity and access management tools
  • Slack or email for internal notifications

Operational Notes

This workflow reduces manual follow-ups while still allowing teams to review, pause, or audit individual steps. Completion tracking is strongly recommended for compliance and reporting purposes.
