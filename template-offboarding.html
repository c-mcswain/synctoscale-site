Overview

The Employee Offboarding template coordinates secure deprovisioning when an employee leaves the organization. Once a termination is recorded, SyncToScale disables identity access, revokes application permissions, and logs every action for audit visibility.

How it works

  1. Termination recorded in HRIS.
  2. Nova validates timing and fields.
  3. Identity and SSO access revoked.
  4. IT and Security tasks created.
  5. Notifications sent to stakeholders.
