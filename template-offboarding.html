Workflow Template

Employee Offboarding Automation

A structured offboarding workflow that coordinates HR, IT, payroll, and internal systems to ensure secure access removal, accurate final payroll, and clean operational handoff.

Category: HR Operations Difficulty: Intermediate Trigger: Termination Event Systems: HRIS, Payroll, IT, Slack

Overview

Offboarding is one of the most operationally sensitive HR workflows. This template ensures that employee exits are handled consistently, securely, and without manual gaps that can lead to compliance or access risks.

The workflow activates when an employee termination is recorded and coordinates actions across people systems and internal tooling.

Primary Triggers

  • Employee termination recorded in HRIS
  • Separation date reached
  • Termination reason updated or confirmed

Key Workflow Actions

  • Deactivate system access and credentials
  • Notify IT and security teams automatically
  • Trigger final payroll processing
  • Reassign owned documents and system responsibilities
  • Log offboarding completion status

Common Integrations

  • HRIS platforms (employee status updates)
  • Payroll systems (final pay calculations)
  • Identity and access management tools
  • Slack or email for internal notifications

Operational Notes

This workflow is designed to reduce reliance on manual checklists while still allowing teams to audit each offboarding step. Logging and completion tracking are recommended for compliance and internal reporting.
