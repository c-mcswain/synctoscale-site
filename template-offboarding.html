Employee Offboarding Automation

A thoughtfully designed offboarding workflow that keeps HR, IT, and payroll in quiet sync — ensuring secure access removal, clean handoffs, and accurate final pay without unnecessary friction.

HR Operations Intermediate Termination Event HRIS · Payroll · IT · Slack

Overview

Offboarding is one of the most sensitive operational moments inside an organization. This workflow ensures employee exits are handled consistently, securely, and without relying on memory or manual checklists.

Once a termination is recorded, the workflow coordinates actions across people systems and internal tooling while maintaining visibility and control.

Primary Triggers

  • Employee termination recorded in the HRIS
  • Scheduled separation date reached
  • Termination details confirmed or updated

Key Workflow Actions

  • Deactivate system access and credentials
  • Notify IT and security teams automatically
  • Trigger final payroll processing
  • Reassign owned documents and responsibilities
  • Log offboarding completion for audit visibility

Common Integrations

  • HRIS platforms for employee status changes
  • Payroll systems for final compensation
  • Identity and access management tools
  • Slack or email for internal notifications

Operational Notes

This workflow minimizes manual follow-ups while still allowing teams to review, pause, or audit individual steps. Completion tracking is recommended for compliance and reporting.
