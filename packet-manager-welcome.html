Your Focus Before Day One

  • Confirm required systems and tools are accessible
  • Schedule first-week meetings and introductions
  • Clarify role expectations and success signals
  • Align initial priorities with team goals

30 / 60 / 90-Day Guidance

First 30 Days — Foundations

  • Learn systems, workflows, and team norms
  • Complete required onboarding tasks
  • Establish a consistent 1:1 cadence

60 Days — Momentum

  • Begin contributing independently
  • Build cross-functional relationships
  • Review performance goals and expectations

90 Days — Ownership

  • Own responsibilities confidently
  • Evaluate progress against role expectations
  • Identify growth and development opportunities

Key Meetings & Milestones

  • Day 1 welcome and team introduction
  • End-of-week check-in
  • 30-day review
  • 60-day progress discussion
  • 90-day milestone review
