Template

Automatically detect promotion, role change, or compensation adjustments within your HRIS and push real-time alerts to managers, payroll, RevOps, and IT. Nova identifies risk patterns and validates missing or unexpected values.

Overview

The Title or Compensation Change Alerts template monitors HRIS updates for sensitive People Ops events such as promotions, lateral moves, salary adjustments, bonus changes, and other compensation-related updates. When a change is detected, SyncToScale identifies what changed, who it impacts, and which teams must be notified.

How it works

  1. An employee profile is updated in the HRIS.
  2. SyncToScale detects specific field changes.
  3. Nova compares old vs new values for anomalies.
  4. Relevant internal teams are notified.
  5. Optional downstream actions are triggered.

Workflow steps

  1. Trigger: HRIS employee updated

    Runs on webhook or API-based employee updates.

  2. Detect field changes

    Monitors job title, department, salary, pay band, and manager.

  3. Nova validation

    Checks approval metadata, ranges, and cross-system consistency.

  4. Notify teams

    Sends alerts to HR, Payroll, IT, and managers.

  5. Audit logging

    Stores change records with Nova confidence scoring.
