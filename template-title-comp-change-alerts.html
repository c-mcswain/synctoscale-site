Title or Compensation Change Alerts

Template

Automatically detect promotion, role change, or compensation adjustments within your HRIS and push real-time alerts to managers, payroll, RevOps, and IT. Nova identifies risk patterns and validates missing or unexpected values.

Overview

The Title or Compensation Change Alerts template monitors HRIS updates for sensitive People Ops events: promotions, lateral moves, salary adjustments, bonus changes, and other compensation-related updates. When a change is detected, SyncToScale identifies what changed, who it impacts, and which teams must be notified.

When to use this template

  • You want instant alerts when job titles or pay bands change.
  • Payroll and IT need to react quickly (system permissions, pay updates, role access, etc.).
  • You want a consistent audit trail for compensation changes.
  • You want Nova to check for anomalies or missing approval fields.

What this template automates

  • Detects HRIS changes on title, job code, salary, or pay band.
  • Sends alerts to HR, Payroll, IT, or managers.
  • Logs updates in an audit system or dashboard.
  • Triggers access reviews or provisioning updates if needed.
  • Uses Nova to validate fields and identify unusual entries.

How it works

  1. An employee profile is updated in the HRIS.
  2. SyncToScale detects specific field changes (title, department, compensation ranges).
  3. Nova compares old vs new fields for anomalies or approval mismatches.
  4. Internal teams are notified with details and next steps.
  5. System updates (permissions, payroll adjustments) can trigger automatically.
