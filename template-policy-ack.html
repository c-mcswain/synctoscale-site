Policy Acknowledgement Tracking

Template

Automate policy distribution and employee acknowledgement tracking across HRIS, Slack/Email, and compliance systems. Nova identifies overdue acknowledgements, risk segments, and engagement gaps.

Category: People Ops & Compliance Difficulty: Beginner Trigger: Policy updates / scheduled reminders Systems: HRIS · Slack · Email · Compliance Platform

Overview

The Policy Acknowledgement Tracking template automatically distributes new or updated policies to employees, tracks who has acknowledged them, sends reminders to those who haven’t, and surfaces compliance risks with Nova’s pattern detection.

How it works

  1. A new policy is published or updated.
  2. SyncToScale builds an acknowledgement roster from the HRIS.
  3. Employees receive an acknowledgement request via Slack or email.
  4. Acknowledgements are tracked in real time.
  5. Nova identifies overdue or at-risk segments.
  6. Managers and Compliance receive rollups.

Set up this template

  1. Open the Templates library.
  2. Locate Policy Acknowledgement Tracking.
  3. Connect HRIS and Slack/Email systems.
  4. Upload policies and define versions.
  5. Configure reminder cadence.
  6. Enable Nova compliance scoring.
  7. Activate the workflow.

Template summary
