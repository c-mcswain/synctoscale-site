Policy Acknowledgement Tracking

Automate policy distribution and employee acknowledgement tracking across HRIS, Slack/Email, and compliance systems — with Nova identifying overdue acknowledgements and compliance risk patterns.

Overview

This template automatically distributes new or updated policies, tracks employee acknowledgements, sends reminders, and generates audit-ready compliance logs — with Nova surfacing risk segments and trends.

How it works

  1. A policy is published or updated.
  2. SyncToScale builds an acknowledgement roster from the HRIS.
  3. Employees receive an acknowledgement request.
  4. Acknowledgements are tracked in real time.
  5. Nova flags overdue or at-risk groups.
  6. Managers and Compliance receive rollups.

Set up this template

  1. Select Policy Acknowledgement Tracking from Templates.
  2. Connect HRIS and Slack/Email systems.
  3. Upload policies and define versions.
  4. Configure reminder cadence.
  5. Enable Nova compliance scoring.
  6. Activate the workflow.

Template summary

  • Trigger: Policy published
  • Runtime: Real-time + scheduled
  • Owner: People Ops · Compliance
  • Systems: HRIS · Slack · Email

Nova will help you…

  • Identify overdue acknowledgements early.
  • Improve audit readiness.
  • Spot compliance risk patterns.
