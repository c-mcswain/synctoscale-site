Policy Acknowledgement Tracking

Automate policy distribution and employee acknowledgement tracking across HRIS, Slack/Email, and compliance systems. Nova identifies overdue acknowledgements, risk segments, and engagement gaps.

Overview

The Policy Acknowledgement Tracking template automatically distributes new or updated policies, tracks acknowledgements, sends reminders to overdue employees, and surfaces compliance risks using Nova’s pattern detection.

How it works

  1. A policy is published or updated.
  2. Target employees are pulled from the HRIS.
  3. Acknowledgement requests are sent via Slack, Email, or HRIS.
  4. Responses are tracked in real time.
  5. Nova flags overdue acknowledgements and risk clusters.
  6. Managers and compliance receive summaries.

Workflow steps

  1. Trigger: Policy publish or update.
  2. Roster build: Active employees only.
  3. Send request: Policy link + acknowledge action.
  4. Track: Timestamped acknowledgements.
  5. Remind: Scheduled follow-ups.
  6. Report: Audit-ready compliance logs.

Field mappings

  • firstName + lastName → Employee name
  • email → Delivery address
  • department → Compliance grouping
  • policyVersion → Audit version tracking
  • timestamp → Acknowledged at

Nova insights

  • Detects departments with high overdue rates.
  • Highlights repeat non-acknowledgers.
  • Predicts overall compliance health.

Set up this template

  1. Select Policy Acknowledgement Tracking from Templates.
  2. Connect HRIS and Slack/Email systems.
  3. Upload policies and define versions.
  4. Configure reminder cadence.
  5. Enable Nova compliance scoring.
  6. Activate workflow.

Template summary

  • Trigger: Policy publish / reminders
  • Runtime: Real-time + scheduled
  • Owner: People Ops · Compliance
  • Systems: HRIS · Slack/Email · Compliance

Best practices

  • Send reminders every 3 days.
  • Include policy summaries in messages.
  • Send manager rollups weekly.

Nova will help you…

  • Identify compliance gaps early.
  • Improve audit readiness.
  • Track acknowledgement trends over time.
