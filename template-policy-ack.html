Overview

The Policy Acknowledgment Confirmation workflow ensures that employees receive, review, and formally acknowledge company policies. Acknowledgments are tracked automatically, reminders are sent to overdue employees, and compliance teams receive a complete audit trail.

When to use this workflow

  • You release or update company policies or handbooks
  • You need proof of acknowledgment for audits or legal review
  • Manual tracking is inconsistent or time-consuming
  • You want early visibility into compliance risk

What this workflow automates

Distribution

  • Slack DM, email, or HRIS task delivery
  • Policy version tracking
  • Employee targeting by role or location

Tracking & Reminders

  • Acknowledgment timestamps
  • Automated reminder cadence
  • Escalation to managers

Compliance Visibility

  • Audit-ready logs
  • Department-level summaries
  • Nova risk pattern detection

High-level workflow

  1. Policy is published or updated
  2. Eligible employees are identified from the HRIS
  3. Acknowledgment requests are sent
  4. Responses are logged in real time
  5. Reminders are issued to overdue employees
  6. Compliance reports are generated

Workflow summary

  • Primary owner: People Ops / Compliance
  • Runtime: Real-time + scheduled
  • Output: Policy acknowledgment confirmation

Nova insights

  • Identify overdue risk segments
  • Highlight departments with low engagement
  • Support audit readiness with confidence
