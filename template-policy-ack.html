When to use this template

  • You release updated policies and need employees to confirm review.
  • Tracking acknowledgements manually is slow or inconsistent.

What this template automates

How it works

  1. A new policy is published, or an existing one is updated.
  2. SyncToScale gathers the target employee list from the HRIS.
  3. Employees receive a message with a link to review and acknowledge.
  4. Responses are tracked in real-time.
  5. Nova identifies overdue acknowledgements or compliance risk clusters.
  6. Managers receive team-level summaries.
  7. Compliance receives audit-ready logs.
