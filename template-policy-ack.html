Policy Acknowledgement Tracking

Template

Automate policy distribution and employee acknowledgement tracking across HRIS, Slack/Email, and compliance systems. Nova identifies overdue acknowledgements, risk segments, and engagement gaps.

Category: People Ops & Compliance Difficulty: Beginner Trigger: Policy updates / scheduled reminders Systems: HRIS · Slack · Email · Compliance Platform

Overview

The Policy Acknowledgement Tracking template automatically distributes new or updated policies to employees, tracks acknowledgements, sends reminders to those who haven’t responded, and provides audit-ready compliance visibility with Nova insights.

How it works

  1. A policy is published or updated.
  2. SyncToScale builds an acknowledgement roster from the HRIS.
  3. Employees receive acknowledgement requests via Slack or email.
  4. Responses are tracked in real time.
  5. Nova highlights overdue acknowledgements and risk patterns.
  6. Managers and Compliance receive rollups and audit logs.

Set up this workflow

  1. Connect your HRIS and Slack/Email systems.
  2. Upload or link the policy documents to be acknowledged.
  3. Define policy versions and acknowledgement requirements.
  4. Configure reminder cadence and escalation rules.
  5. Enable Nova compliance scoring and risk insights.
  6. Activate the workflow.

Nova insights for this template

  • Identifies departments with high overdue acknowledgement rates.
  • Detects repeat non-responders and compliance risk clusters.
  • Predicts overall policy compliance health.
  • Supports audit readiness with explainable tracking.
