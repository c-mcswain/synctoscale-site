Policy Acknowledgement Tracking

Template

Automate policy distribution and employee acknowledgement tracking across HRIS, Slack/Email, and compliance systems. Nova identifies overdue acknowledgements, risk segments, and engagement gaps.

Category: People Ops & Compliance Difficulty: Beginner Trigger: Policy updates / scheduled reminders Systems: HRIS · Slack · Email · Compliance Platform

Overview

The Policy Acknowledgement Tracking template automatically distributes new or updated policies to employees, tracks who has acknowledged them, sends reminders to those who haven’t, and surfaces compliance risks with Nova’s pattern detection. Useful for handbooks, security policies, code of conduct acknowledgements, and compliance training requirements.

When to use this template

  • You release updated policies and need employees to confirm review.
  • Tracking acknowledgements manually is slow or inconsistent.
  • Audits require documentation of who signed and when.
  • You want Nova to identify at-risk segments or overdue employees.

What this template automates

  • Policy distribution via Slack, email, or HRIS inbox.
  • Tracking acknowledgement timestamps.
  • Automatic reminders to overdue employees.
  • Manager rollups so they can follow up with their teams.
  • Nova risk scoring for compliance gaps.
  • End-to-end audit trail logs.

How it works

  1. A new policy is published, or an existing one is updated.
  2. SyncToScale gathers the target employee list from the HRIS.
  3. Employees receive a message with a link to review and acknowledge.
  4. Responses are tracked in real-time.
  5. Nova identifies overdue acknowledgements or compliance risk clusters.
  6. Managers receive team-level summaries.
  7. Compliance receives audit-ready logs.

Workflow steps

  1. Step 1 — Trigger: Policy published or updated

    Triggered via manual publish, HRIS event, or integration with compliance platforms (like Drata, Vanta, or internal SharePoint updates).

  2. Step 2 — Build acknowledgement roster

    Collects:

    • Employee name, email
    • Manager
    • Department
    • Location / timezone
    • Employment status

  3. Step 3 — Send policy acknowledgement request

    Delivery options include:

    • Slack DM
    • Email
    • HRIS inbox task
    Message includes:
    • Policy title + version
    • Summary of updates
    • Link to read full policy
    • “Acknowledge” button

  4. Step 4 — Track acknowledgements

    SyncToScale listens for acknowledgement events and logs timestamps per employee.

  5. Step 5 — Remind overdue employees

    Scheduled reminders continue until acknowledgement is completed.

  6. Step 6 — Send manager rollups

    Weekly or daily summaries showing which employees on each team have not yet acknowledged.

  7. Step 7 — Compliance reporting

    Audit-friendly records are generated for compliance teams.

Error handling

  • If employee email is missing, send acknowledgement request to manager.
  • If policy link is unavailable, retries occur every 15 minutes.
  • HRIS sync issues send alerts to People Ops.
  • Duplicate acknowledgements are ignored but logged.

Example error

{
  "runId": "run_01J2POLICYAUDIT",
  "status": "warning",
  "message": "Employees missing active email addresses: 4"
}
