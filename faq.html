General questions

No — SyncToScale is a concept project. The goal is to present a believable AI automation platform with realistic copy, feature design, and structure, without a full production backend.

It’s aimed at:

  • Recruiters and hiring managers in SaaS / AI / workflow automation.
  • Teams curious how a TAM / CS / implementation-minded person thinks about product and process.
  • Anyone who wants to see a “fake but plausible” AI platform end to end.

Platform & features

The platform is modeled around:

  • Customer onboarding and implementation projects.
  • Account health monitoring and renewal playbooks.
  • Support escalations and cross-functional handoffs.
  • Internal operations tasks that touch multiple systems.

Nova is positioned as a sidekick — not a replacement for humans. Conceptually, Nova would:

  • Summarize long customer histories into actionable notes.
  • Suggest routing, tags, and next steps for incoming requests.
  • Help draft communications, while humans approve and send.

All of this is framed around human-in-the-loop guardrails, not “fire-and-forget” automation.

In this portfolio version, the integrations are conceptual only. The UI and copy are built as if real connectors exist, to demonstrate how I’d name, position, and explain them to customers.

For recruiters & hiring managers

It reflects my tendency to:

  • Think in systems and cross-functional workflows.
  • Translate technical ideas into clear, customer-friendly language.
  • Design for TAM / CS / implementation teams who live in multiple tools all day.

In previous roles, I’ve used AI tools to:

  • Summarize calls and highlight follow-ups.
  • Draft customer comms while preserving voice and nuance.
  • Spot patterns in tickets or account risk signals.

SyncToScale is a way to package that mindset in a single, cohesive story.
