About SyncToScale

A fictional AI automation platform designed to look and feel like a real SaaS product — built to showcase how I think about workflow design, customer experience, and ops-friendly AI.

Created by Christy McSwain · Technical Account Manager / Implementation-minded nerd.
What this project represents

Why build a pretend AI platform?

My day-to-day work lives at the intersection of customer experience, operations, and tooling. SyncToScale is a sandbox to show how I’d shape an AI-powered workflow product for real teams.

  • Translate messy, cross-functional workflows into clear, reusable playbooks.
  • Highlight the kinds of automations TAMs, CSMs, and implementation managers actually need.
  • Demonstrate comfort with technical concepts (integrations, data flow, guardrails) in human language.
  • Showcase product sense: positioning, pricing, docs, FAQ, and resource design.
Under the hood

What’s real vs. concept

The UI and copy are real; the backend is intentionally minimal. Think of this as a narrative demo of how I’d support and shape an AI platform alongside Product, Eng, and Operations.

For recruiters & hiring managers

How this connects to my experience

I’ve spent years in HR tech and SaaS roles (TAM, customer success, implementation-adjacent support), where “automation” is only useful if it respects human nuance and process reality.

  • Experience mapping messy workflows for CS, Support, and RevOps teams.
  • Comfort translating between technical constraints and business requirements.
  • Hands-on work improving routing, queue design, and tooling adoption.
  • Practical use of AI tools to summarize, triage, and coach — not replace — human work.
If you’re skimming: this project is my way of saying “I understand the problems an AI automation platform is trying to solve — and I know how to support customers, internal teams, and product decisions around it.”
