Title & Compensation Change Alerts

Template

Automatically detect job title changes, compensation adjustments, and role updates in the HRIS — then notify Payroll, Finance, IT, and Managers. Nova highlights potential errors, inconsistencies, or missing approvals.

Category: People Ops & Compliance Difficulty: Intermediate Trigger: HRIS employee.updated Systems: HRIS · Slack · Email · Payroll · Identity Access

Overview

The Title & Compensation Change Alerts template automatically identifies changes to job titles, compensation bands, pay rates, job levels, FLSA status, and promotions. It alerts the relevant teams while Nova analyzes potential issues, such as unapproved changes, compensation outliers, or mismatched job levels.

When to use this template

  • Payroll or Finance often receives late notification of comp changes.
  • Managers forget to follow approval workflows.
  • You want automated validation for compliance-sensitive fields.
  • You need a consistent record of employee changes across teams.

What this template automates

  • Detects HRIS updates that modify key fields (title, salary, FLSA, level).
  • Sends Slack or email alerts to Payroll, Finance, and IT.
  • Surfaces Nova insights about inconsistencies or missing approvals.
  • Logs changes for audits & comp cycle reporting.
  • Optionally triggers IT access review or updated provisioning.

How it works

  1. An employee's record is updated in the HRIS.
  2. SyncToScale compares new vs. previous values.
  3. Nova evaluates whether the change appears valid or risky.
  4. Alerts are sent to the correct teams with a detailed summary.
  5. Optional downstream updates (payroll system, access changes) run automatically.

Workflow steps

  1. Step 1 — Trigger: HRIS employee.updated

    Runs when fields such as jobTitle, compensationRate, jobLevel, or flsaStatus are modified.

  2. Step 2 — Detect field changes

    SyncToScale automatically compares:

    • Old vs. new title
    • Old vs. new salary/hourly rate
    • Old vs. new job level
    • Old vs. new department
    • Old vs. new FLSA/exempt status

  3. Step 3 — Nova compensation & title validation

    Nova analyzes:

    • Whether the change fits standard compensation bands
    • Whether approval workflow is missing
    • Whether job level changes align with role expectations
    • Is comp adjustment unusually high/low for the tier?

  4. Step 4 — Send alerts

    Slack/Email alerts are sent to:

    • Payroll
    • Finance
    • IT (for access review)
    • Manager
    • People Ops

  5. Step 5 — Optional downstream updates

    Automated adjustments may include:

    • Updating payroll or compensation systems
    • Triggering an IT access review ticket
    • Updating managerial permissions or system roles

Field mappings

HRIS → Alert summary

HRIS Field Transform Alert Field
jobTitle Compare old vs. new Updated Title
compensationRate Normalize currency New Compensation
jobLevel Convert numeric/string discrepancies New Level
department Normalize labeling Department
flsaStatus Standardize values (“Exempt”, “Non-Exempt”) FLSA Status

Nova validation results

Nova Signal Description Impact
Band alignment Checks if new comp fits role range Compliance + Payroll
Approval missing No workflow detected Manager + HR review

Sample JSON payloads

Trigger event — HRIS employee.updated

{
  "eventType": "employee.updated",
  "employeeId": "EMP-4411",
  "changes": {
    "old": {
      "jobTitle": "Support Specialist",
      "compensationRate": 64000,
      "jobLevel": 2,
      "flsaStatus": "Non-Exempt"
    },
    "new": {
      "jobTitle": "Support Team Lead",
      "compensationRate": 78000,
      "jobLevel": 3,
      "flsaStatus": "Exempt"
    }
  }
}

Error handling

  • If required fields are missing, Nova flags the change and halts notifications.
  • If payroll sync fails, retry + notify Payroll channel.
  • If IT access review cannot be created, send fallback alert.
  • All changes are logged for audit readiness.

Sample error

{
  "runId": "run_01J2TITLECOMP",
  "status": "warning",
