Stale Opportunities Digest

Template

Automatically surface aging pipeline deals that haven’t been touched, updated, or progressed. Send weekly digests to reps and managers — with Nova highlighting risk, next steps, and ICP fit.

Category: CRM & RevOps Difficulty: Beginner Trigger: Scheduled (Weekly) Systems: CRM · Slack · Email · Nova

Overview

The Stale Opportunities Digest template automatically compiles a weekly summary of pipeline opportunities that haven’t moved, haven’t been contacted recently, or show signs of going cold. Nova reviews each deal for risk, identifies possible next steps, and surfaces which reps or segments need the most attention.

When to use this template

  • Your pipeline reviews rely on manual spreadsheets or ad-hoc digging.
  • Reps forget to update opportunities regularly.
  • Sales managers want proactive visibility into aging deals.
  • You want Nova to help prioritize follow-up actions.

What this template automates

  • Weekly scan for stale, inactive, or aging opportunities.
  • Summaries delivered to AEs, SDRs, and managers.
  • Nova commentary on risk, recommended actions, and likelihood of revival.
  • Pipeline hygiene insights for RevOps.
  • Optional auto-reminders for reps with lots of stale pipeline.

How it works

  1. A weekly scheduled job queries CRM for all open opportunities.
  2. SyncToScale filters deals meeting “stale” criteria.
  3. Nova analyzes risk indicators and suggests next actions.
  4. A digest is generated, grouped by rep or segment.
  5. Slack/Email sends the digest automatically.
  6. RevOps receives a macro-level summary of pipeline hygiene.

Workflow steps

  1. Step 1 — Scheduled weekly trigger

    Workflow runs weekly (default: Mondays 8 AM). Adjustable to any cadence.

  2. Step 2 — Query opportunities

    Filters include:

    • No activity in X days (default: 14)
    • No stage movement in X days (default: 21)
    • Past close date
    • Low engagement score (optional)

  3. Step 3 — Nova stale-deal evaluation

    Nova scores each deal on:

    • Risk of loss
    • ICP fit
    • Engagement trends
    • Recommmended next steps
    • Opportunity age vs. typical win cycle

  4. Step 4 — Build digest

    Deals are grouped by owner, segment, or region and assembled into Slack/email digest format.

  5. Step 5 — Send notifications

    AEs and SDRs receive personalized lists; managers receive rollups.

  6. Step 6 — Log insights

    Summary metrics are written to CRM or an analytics warehouse for pipeline health reporting.

Error handling

  • If CRM API fails, run resumes automatically in next scheduled window.
  • Missing rep assignments → notify Sales Ops.
  • Opportunities with invalid dates are excluded and logged.
  • Nova risk scores fallback to “Unknown” if analytics unavailable.

  • Improves forecast accuracy by addressing stale deals early.

Set up this template

  1. Select Stale Opportunities Digest in your Templates Library.
  2. Connect your CRM + Slack/Email tools.
  3. Define stale criteria (no activity in X days, etc.).
  4. Configure digest grouping (by rep, region, segment).
  5. Enable Nova risk evaluation and suggestions.
  6. Turn on weekly schedule.

Best practice defaults

  • Consider 14 days of inactivity as “stale.”
  • Group digest by rep for clarity.
  • Only send manager digests for deals above a value threshold.
