Churn Risk Alerts

Template

Combine product usage, tickets, billing, and NPS into a single health score. Proactively alert CSMs when accounts show early churn signals.

Category: Customer Success Difficulty: Intermediate Trigger: Scheduled scan Systems: Product Analytics · Helpdesk · Billing · CRM

Overview

The Churn Risk Alerts template helps Customer Success teams monitor account health across multiple tools. On a set schedule (e.g., daily), SyncToScale pulls signals from your product analytics, helpdesk, billing system, and NPS surveys. Nova combines them into a simple risk score and alerts the owning CSM when thresholds are crossed.

When to use this template

  • You track usage, tickets, and revenue but don’t have a unified health signal.
  • CSMs manage too many accounts to manually check each one weekly.
  • You want a lightweight, explainable alternative to heavy CS tooling.
  • You need clear, auditable criteria for churn risk and save plays.

What this template automates

  • Scheduled health scoring for selected accounts.
  • Combines product usage, support volume, NPS, and billing status.
  • Creates CSM tasks in your CRM or project tool.
  • Sends rich Slack alerts with Nova’s recommended next steps.

How it works

  1. A scheduled trigger runs (e.g., daily at 08:00 in your chosen timezone).
  2. SyncToScale loads active accounts and their CSM owners from the CRM.
  3. For each account, it pulls usage, ticket volume, NPS, and billing status.
  4. Nova calculates a risk score and classifies the account (Healthy, Watch, At-risk).
  5. At-risk accounts generate tasks and Slack alerts for the owning CSM.
  6. Nova tracks how risk scores improve (or worsen) after follow-up actions.

Workflow steps

  1. Step 1 — Trigger: Scheduled run

    This template is designed to run on a schedule, such as daily or weekly. You can configure the cadence under Automation > Schedules in SyncToScale.

  2. Step 2 — Load target accounts

    SyncToScale queries your CRM for active subscriptions, filtered by owner, segment, or ARR threshold (e.g., “All accounts > $10k ARR”).

  3. Step 3 — Pull signals from tools

    For each account, we gather:

    • Usage: logins, key events, or seats used (from Amplitude, Mixpanel, Segment, etc.).
    • Support: ticket count and CSAT over the last 30 days.
    • NPS / surveys: most recent score and trend.
    • Billing: past-due invoices or downgrades (Stripe, Chargebee, Recurly, etc.).

    Behind the scenes — Nova scoring

    Nova uses a simple, explainable scoring model you can adjust:

    • Usage change vs. baseline (−40% may be a risk flag).
    • Ticket surge + low CSAT = higher risk.
    • Recent NPS detractor always increases risk.
    • Billing issues raise risk and surface in the alert.

  4. Step 4 — Calculate risk score

    Each account gets a numeric score (0–100) and a label: Healthy, Watch, or At-risk.

  5. Step 5 — Create tasks & alerts

    For accounts marked At-risk, SyncToScale:

    • Creates a follow-up task or call in the CRM.
    • Posts a detailed Slack message with context and Nova’s suggestions.
    • Optionally adds a “Churn Risk” tag or custom field for reporting.

Account.Domain__c or Website Normalize domain Used as key for analytics workspace / tenant
Account.Owner__r.Email Map to internal user / Slack handle Used in alert recipient and task owner

Signals → Health score

Signal Example rule Score impact
Usage drop 30-day active users down >= 40% vs. prior 30 days −30 points
Ticket surge Tickets in last 14 days > 3× account baseline −20 points
NPS detractor Most recent NPS <= 6 −25 points
Billing issues Invoice past due > 14 days −25 points
Positive usage trend Usage up >= 30% vs. baseline +20 points

Error handling

This template is resilient to partial data gaps. If one signal source isn’t reachable, Nova will still generate a score with adjusted confidence.

  • Missing analytics data: risk score falls back to support + NPS + billing.
  • Billing system offline: a warning is added to Nova diagnostics, score is flagged as partial.
  • CRM task creation failures are retried and surfaced in a summary report.

Nova insights for this template

  • Shows which signals most frequently drive accounts into “At-risk”.
  • Highlights accounts that stayed at risk for multiple runs without follow-up.
  • Tracks how scores change after CSM calls or QBRs are logged.
  • Suggests new risk rules based on your historical churn patterns.

Set up this template in SyncToScale

  1. Select Churn Risk Alerts from the Templates library.
  2. Connect your CRM, product analytics, helpdesk, NPS, and billing tools.
  3. Choose which segments / ARR bands to include in the scan.
  4. Adjust scoring weights and thresholds to fit your business.
  5. Connect Slack and pick the channel for daily summaries.
  6. Enable the daily schedule and review Nova’s dashboard after the first few runs.
