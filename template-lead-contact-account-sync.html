Lead → Contact → Account Sync

Template

Automatically convert leads, attach them to the correct accounts, enrich CRM records, and prevent duplicates using Nova’s intelligent matching and normalization.

Category: CRM & RevOps Difficulty: Intermediate Trigger: Lead created or updated Systems: CRM · Enrichment API · Slack · Email

Overview

The Lead → Contact → Account Sync template automates a clean and consistent lead conversion process. When new leads enter the CRM, SyncToScale checks for duplicates, enriches them with external firmographic data, normalizes fields, and converts the lead into a Contact + Account with directional rules powered by Nova.

When to use this template

  • Your CRM has duplicate lead and contact records.
  • Sales reps manually convert leads inconsistently.
  • Marketing → Sales handoff often breaks due to missing fields.
  • You want Nova to detect merge candidates and auto-route to correct accounts.

What this template automates

  • Lead normalization (email, domain, capitalization, phone).
  • Duplicate detection using Nova match logic.
  • Lead conversion to Contact + Account in Salesforce or HubSpot.
  • Account attachment rules (existing or new).
  • Slack alerts for high-value or strategic accounts.
  • Complete logging for RevOps and audit trails.

How it works

  1. A lead is created or updated in the CRM (Salesforce / HubSpot).
  2. SyncToScale enriches the record (domain → company info).
  3. Nova checks for duplicate contacts or accounts.
  4. If a match exists → attach lead to existing account and update fields.
  5. If no match → create new Account + Contact automatically.
  6. Run normalization rules and push back to CRM.
  7. Alert Sales or RevOps for high-value or mismatched domain leads.

Workflow steps

  1. Step 1 — Trigger: Lead created/updated

    Triggered via CRM webhook or scheduled polling. Filters can restrict events to only marketing-sourced leads or those with required fields (email).

  2. Step 2 — Normalize lead fields

    SyncToScale applies transformations such as:

    • Email → lowercase & domain extraction
    • Phone → E.164 format
    • Name → title case
    • Company → trim punctuation and normalize

  3. Step 3 — Nova deduplication

    Nova evaluates the lead against existing Contacts and Accounts with multi-factor scoring:

    • Email match (primary and secondary)
    • Domain match + name similarity
    • Phone number match
    • Intent signals from marketing tools

  4. Step 4 — Convert lead

    Depending on the outcome:

    • Match found: Convert lead and attach to the existing account.
    • No match: Create new account using enriched firmographics.

  5. Step 5 — Enrich data

    SyncToScale calls enrichment APIs (Clearbit, Apollo, etc.) to populate:

    • Company size
    • Industry
    • Tech stack
    • Revenue estimates

  6. Step 6 — Alert Sales

    Alerts are sent when:

    • Lead belongs to a strategic account
    • Domain mismatch suggests personal or disposable email
    • Nova detects multiple possible account matches

Error handling

  • If email is missing, Nova marks the record for manual review.
  • If multiple account matches exist, the workflow pauses and alerts RevOps.
  • If enrichment fails, conversion proceeds with CRM-only fields.
  • Duplicate conversions are prevented by ID locking.

Set up this template

  1. Select Lead → Contact → Account Sync in your Templates Library.
  2. Connect your CRM + Enrichment API providers.
  3. Define duplicate detection tolerance (match scoring rules).
  4. Customize conversion rules for strategic accounts.
  5. Enable Nova dedupe + normalization logic.
  6. Turn on Active mode.
