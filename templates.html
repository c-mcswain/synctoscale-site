Template Examples

Salesforce → Slack Lead Alert


Trigger: lead.created (Salesforce)
Action: send_message (Slack)

Message Template:
"New lead: {{payload.name}} from {{payload.company}}"

Jira → Google Sheet Sync


Trigger: issue.created (Jira)
Action: append_row (Google Sheets)

Columns:
- Issue ID
- Summary
- Priority
- Assignee

HubSpot Deal Stage Email


Trigger: deal.stage_changed (HubSpot)
Action: send_email (Gmail)

Template:
"Deal {{payload.name}} moved to {{payload.stage}}"
