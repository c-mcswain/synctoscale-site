Workflow Templates

Jump-start your automations with prebuilt templates for onboarding, support, CRM sync, IT provisioning, and people operations—powered by SyncToScale and Nova.

Employee Onboarding

New Hire → IT Provisioning

When a new employee is created in your HRIS, automatically create accounts in Google Workspace, Slack, and your ticketing tool, then notify IT.

  • Trigger: employee.created (HRIS)
  • Systems: BambooHR / Gusto → Google Workspace / Slack / Jira
  • Highlights: Field mapping, conditional role assignment, Nova validation

New Hire → Manager Welcome Packet

Generate a summary for the hiring manager with role, comp band, start date, and links to key systems.

  • Trigger: employee.created
  • Systems: HRIS → Slack / Email
  • Highlights: Data normalization, Nova-generated summary text

Pre-Start Checklist

Run a nightly check for upcoming start dates and ensure devices, access, and orientation tasks are ready.

  • Trigger: scheduled (daily)
  • Systems: HRIS → Jira / Asana
  • Highlights: Scheduled triggers, exception reporting

Support & Customer Success

VIP Ticket Routing

Route tickets from high-value accounts to a dedicated queue with Slack alerts for the owning CSM or TAM.

  • Trigger: ticket.created (Zendesk/Freshdesk)
  • Systems: Support → CRM → Slack
  • Highlights: Account lookup, priority tagging, Nova-driven escalation

Churn Risk Signals

Flag accounts with spikes in support volume and send a weekly digest to the success team.

  • Trigger: scheduled (weekly)
  • Systems: Support → CRM → Email/Slack
  • Highlights: Nova anomaly detection, grouped insights

Post-Implementation Check-In

After a go-live milestone, automatically create a check-in task and send a satisfaction survey.

  • Trigger: project.status = “live”
  • Systems: PM tool → CRM / Email
  • Highlights: Multi-system updates, timeline-based triggers

CRM & RevOps

Lead → Contact → Account Sync

Normalize lead data from marketing tools into CRM contacts and accounts, preventing duplicates.

  • Trigger: lead.created (HubSpot/Forms)
  • Systems: Forms / HubSpot → Salesforce
  • Highlights: Upserts, deduplication, Nova mapping suggestions

Contract Signed → Internal Handoff

When a deal is marked Closed Won, create implementation tasks and notify the onboarding team.

  • Trigger: deal.closed_won
  • Systems: CRM → PM tool / Slack
  • Highlights: Role-based routing, templated tasks

Stale Opportunities Digest

Generate a list of stalled opportunities and send a weekly summary to sales leadership.

  • Trigger: scheduled (weekly)
  • Systems: CRM → Email
  • Highlights: Date filters, Nova-prioritized list

Policy Acknowledgement Tracking
